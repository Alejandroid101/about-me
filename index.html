My autobiography
I was born in the skirts of the Sierra Madre Oriental mountain, in a small and hot town called Ciudad Victoria, in the state of Tamaulipas, Mexico in the summer of 1991.
Nothing really exciting happened until I was 8 years old, that's when i signed up to Karate. I quickly advanced levels by winning kata and kumite tournaments(belts) until i reached my second degree black-belt in Goju-Ryu
style Karate-Do (same style Dwight Schrute from "The Office" trains).
I went to a couple of universities for 2 different engineering degrees; Mechatronics and Telematics. I was young and wanted to make money so i dropped-out and moved to Cancun to work and party. Little did i know money
doesn't come by easily to those without experience or special skills and talents. I started working as a Time-Share sales rep, but i had no experience, only a little bit of english, which ended up being enough to land the position,
but not to really make a living out of a comission-only position. I finally gave up and moved back to my hometown a year after, put on a suit and got a job in banking; I hated it.
